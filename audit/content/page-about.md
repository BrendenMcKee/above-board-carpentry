## Content (HTML)
<p>Above Board Carpentry is located in the Haliburton Highlands. Graham Borgdorff, owner, has over 20 years of building experience.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Above Board Carpentry is a certified Passive House Tradesperson, is a NetZero Trained Builder as well as being a Red Seal Licence General Carpenter with the RenoMark accreditation.</p>
<p>We are committed to building high-performance homes with the best products and systems available to provide you with a home that is not only stunning,&nbsp;but that also performs at an extremely low operation cost. This certification has given Above Board Carpentry superior knowledge of building science.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Graham and his team also have experience in traditional hand-cut custom timber framing. An example of this can be seen at the entrance to the new tourism Welcome Centre in Haliburton.&nbsp;</p>
