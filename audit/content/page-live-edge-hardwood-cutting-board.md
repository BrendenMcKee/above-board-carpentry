<h2>Maple Cutting Boards &#8211; $50</h2>



<p>Maple is a hardwood with over 125 species of trees. It is a closed-pore hardwood, making it ideal for a cutting board. It is more white in colour with dark brown grain usually in the middle of the cross section.  Each piece is unique and the darker grains will vary piece to piece.</p>



<p><a href="https://aboveboardcarpentry.com/contact/">Contact us</a> to make a purchase; pick up at our home-office or shipping is available.<br>If you prefer one board&#8217;s character over another, please tell us the name of that board in your order.</p>

<h2>Product Information </h2>



<p>These custom cutting boards are cross cuts from hardwood logs: maple and ash. Our boards have kept most of their bark through the milling process, giving them rustic charm.</p>



<p>Our boards will be similar to the pictures above; the pictures are meant to be a sample of what the maple or ash cutting boards will look like. Working with natural wood doesn&#8217;t allow us to have too many pieces that are exactly the same; each board will vary in size, vary in their appearance, and will vary with their natural character. Wood has imperfections, but just like people, we are accepting of all imperfections &#8211; after all, it&#8217;s the imperfections that create some of the most stunning pieces. </p>



<p>We think this board is ideal for everyday use such as a serving platter or for adding rustic charm to your dinner party.</p>



<p>If you would like a special touch to your board, we will brand your board with our logo at no additional cost. When you place your order, please let us know if you are interested.</p>
