<p>Above Board Carpentry is focused on well built homes that provide clients with exceptional comfort, health, and energy saving.&nbsp; We do this by continually educating ourselves on current building science to ensure we deliver the best possible product.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>



<p>ABC primarily builds for residential construction. We are experts in all aspects of a project right from the ground work, including the foundation, right through to the interior finishing&#8217;s.  We have an abundance of experience in project management and would be happy to work with you to work through all the stages of a project. </p>
