## What they do

Above Board Carpentry is a **high-performance residential builder** in the Haliburton Highlands. Their differentiator is not generic carpentry — it is **building science expertise**: Passive House, Net Zero, and energy-efficient construction backed by formal certifications.

**Core services:**
- New residential builds (full project management, foundation to finish)
- Significant renovations and cottage work
- Garages and outbuildings
- Decks, kitchens, bathrooms, bunkies
- Traditional hand-cut custom timber framing
- The ABC Store — live-edge hardwood cutting boards and care products

**Key credentials to feature prominently:**
- Red Seal General Carpenter
- Certified Passive House Tradesperson
- Net Zero Trained Builder (CHBA)
- RenoMark Certified
- Member: OHBA, Passive Buildings Canada

## Brand voice & messaging

**Tone:** Professional, knowledgeable, community-rooted, faith-and-family oriented (subtle, not preachy).

**Recurring themes:**
- High performance / low operating cost
- Comfort, health, and energy savings
- Building science and continuous education
- Craftsmanship and timber framing heritage
- Local Haliburton identity
- Stunning homes that perform

**Sample copy (homepage):**
> "Graham is a red seal certified carpenter, certified passive house tradesperson and committed to building high performance homes with the best products and systems available to provide you with a home that is not only stunning, but with an extremely low operating cost."

## Current site weaknesses (rebrand opportunities)

1. **Generic WordPress theme** — Twenty Seventeen reads as a template, not a premium builder
2. **Dated last-updated feel** — most content unchanged since 2021
3. **Tiny inline images on homepage** — strong photos displayed at 116px–183px wide
4. **Deep, cluttered navigation** — 4 levels in places; legal pages in main nav
5. **Facebook feed as "Current Projects"** — entire page is a plugin embed; fragile and off-brand
6. **No clear primary CTA** — contact is buried; no "Get a Quote" flow
7. **Passive House expertise underplayed** — buried in body copy, not a hero message
8. **ABC Store feels bolted on** — secondary business line needs its own visual lane without competing with construction
9. **Mobile/responsive** — theme is responsive but layout is not optimized for modern mobile-first patterns
10. **SEO/schema** — basic Yoast setup; opportunity for richer local business and project schema

## Brand strengths to preserve

- Genuine, trust-building photography
- Strong certification stack (rare for a regional builder)
- Named portfolio projects (Kash, Bob, Drag) — humanizes the work
- Clear geographic identity (Haliburton Highlands)
- Owner-led story — Graham's expertise and teaching background
- Dual revenue stream (construction + ABC Store) is unique

## Competitive positioning (recommended)

Position as: **"Haliburton's certified high-performance home builder"** — not just another carpenter.

Lead with Passive House / Net Zero credentials, support with portfolio proof, close with local trust and contact.
